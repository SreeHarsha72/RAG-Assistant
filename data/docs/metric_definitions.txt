Metric Definitions

Gross Revenue:
Total order value before discounts, refunds, or adjustments.

Net Revenue:
Gross revenue minus discounts and refunds. In the fct_orders model, this is calculated as:
gross_revenue - discount_amount - refund_amount.

Active Customer:
A customer with at least one completed order in the last 30 days.

Churned Customer:
A customer whose last order was more than 60 days ago.
